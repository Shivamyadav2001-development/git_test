Delicious Burger Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 aloo tikki
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices of cheese (optional)
  • Lettuce, tomato, onion, and pickles (for topping)
  • Ketchup and mustard (for serving)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill or stovetop pan over medium-high heat.
  2. In a bowl, mix the ground beef, salt, and pepper. Form into 4 patties.
  3. Grill the patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until cooked to your liking.
  4. If using cheese, place a slice on each patty during the last minute of cooking to melt.
  5. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill for about 1 minute.
  6. Assemble your burger with the cooked patty and your choice of toppings.
  7. Serve with ketchup and mustard!
