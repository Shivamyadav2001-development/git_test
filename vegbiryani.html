Veg Biryani

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, peas, beans)
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 green chilies
  • Spices (bay leaf, cardamom, cloves)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander and mint

Instructions

  1. Rinse the basmati rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Soak it in water for 30 minutes, then drain.
  2. In a large pot, heat oil or ghee over medium heat. Add the whole spices and saut√© until fragrant.
  3. Add the sliced onions and cook until they are golden brown.
  4. Stir in the ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Cook for another minute.
  5. Add the mixed vegetables and cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Add the soaked and drained rice to the pot. Gently stir to mix everything.
  7. Add water and salt to taste. Bring it to a boil, then cover and reduce the heat to low.
  8. Cook for 15-20 minutes, or until the rice is fully cooked and the water is absorbed.
  9. Once done, fluff the rice with a fork and garnish with fresh coriander and mint.
  10. Serve hot with raita or your favorite side dish.
