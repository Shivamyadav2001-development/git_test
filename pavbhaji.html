Pav Bhaji

Ingredients

  • 4 Pav (bread rolls)
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (potato, peas, carrot, etc.)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 2 tbsp Pav Bhaji masala
  • Butter
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Boil the mixed vegetables (potatoes, peas, carrots) until soft. Drain and mash them in a bowl.
  2. In a large pan, heat 2 tbsp of butter over medium heat. Add chopped onions and saut√© until translucent.
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they are soft and mushy.
  4. Stir in the mashed vegetables and mix well.
  5. Add 2 tbsp of Pav Bhaji masala and salt to taste. Mix thoroughly and let it cook for about 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. In a separate pan, heat some butter and toast the Pav until golden brown on both sides.
  7. Serve the hot Bhaji with buttered Pav, garnished with fresh coriander and lemon wedges.
