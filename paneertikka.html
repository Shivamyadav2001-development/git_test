Paneer Tikka

Ingredients

  • 250g Paneer (cottage cheese)
  • 1 bell pepper, cubed
  • 1 onion, cubed
  • 2 tbsp yogurt
  • 1 tbsp tikka masala
  • Skewers

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, tikka masala, ginger-garlic paste, and salt to create a marinade.
  2. Add the cubed paneer, bell pepper, and onion to the marinade. Mix well and let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat your grill or oven to 200°C (400°F).
  4. Thread the marinated paneer and vegetables onto skewers alternately.
  5. Place the skewers on the grill or in the oven. Brush with oil and cook for about 15-20 minutes, turning occasionally, until golden brown.
  6. Serve hot with lemon wedges and fresh mint chutney.
